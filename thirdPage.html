Materials & items needed!

Below is a list of things you may need for cross country and practices (in no particular order)

  • WATER (It cannot be stressed enough how hydrated and prepared you need to be in order to be able to run long distances & sprints)
  • Appropriate workout attire: spikes & comfortable shoes
  • (optional) Timer device watches or a phone application that tracks your pace and times *This is a great opportunity to set goals for yourself based on your times and progress
